# Positioning & Messaging Decisions

---

## Core Product Positioning

### TrackonAI
**Approved positioning (from finalized Creatify.ai video script, Oct 2025):**

> "TrackonAI is enterprise Commodity Trading and Risk Management software built specifically for physical commodity traders. Not configured ERP modules."

Key message hierarchy (in order):
1. You're losing margin to operational inefficiency — manual reconciliation, delayed exposure reporting, credit checks in Excel, document verification taking days
2. TrackonAI is purpose-built for physical commodity traders (esp. soft commodities: coffee, cotton, cocoa, sugar)
3. TradingDocs.AI is natively integrated — upload docs, verification happens automatically
4. Approval workflows run exactly how you design them
5. AI analyzes every step and flags issues before they become costly
6. Amazon Verified Permissions for enterprise security
7. Excel mode for bulk data entry; custom reports you design yourself

**Contrast framing (what we're NOT):**
- Not a "configured ERP" (direct shot at SAP/generic ERP)
- Not trying to be everything to everyone
- Not for companies that can live with spreadsheets

**Target audience for video:** Business owners of physical commodity trading companies, especially soft commodity traders. "If you're still running on spreadsheets, you're taking risks you can't afford."

---

### TradingDocs.AI
- Positioned as **natively integrated** into TrackonAI, not a standalone product in deals with smaller prospects
- For Salvia 7: LC Comparison feature explicitly moved from TradingDocs.AI into TrackonAI as a built-in — removes a pricing line item and reduces complexity perception
- AI quality self-assessment (internal): 7/10 for LC validation — strong on technical compliance and format validation; weaker on real-world document examination and country-specific regulations. Best use: initial review + training, always supplemented by human expert for actual transactions

---

### Sanctions Screening
- Positioned as vessel AND counterparty screening
- Soft limit: 100 checks/month
- Standalone option exists: $149 Basic TrackonAI + $499 Sanctions = $648/month (for sanctions-only entry point)
- **Note for proposals:** Don't include Sanctions in a proposal if it wasn't demonstrated in the demo — creates surprise and raises friction

## Competitive Messaging

### vs. Generic IT Vendors (Etiya, etc.)
Approved message (WhatsApp-style, to be relayed by champion):

> "IT firmaları genel olarak 'biz yaparız' der, yapabilirler de bazı şeyleri. Ama trading'i bilmeden kurduğun sistem zamanla seni sınırlar. Uzun vadede destek, geliştirme, yeni ihtiyaçlar… bunları trading deneyimi olmadan yönetmek çok zor. Bu kararı sadece fiyata göre vermemelerini tavsiye ederim."

**Never name Etiya directly.** Let the champion relay the logic. Plant doubt about long-term support, not short-term capability.

**Key differentiator to emphasize:** All customers are real trading companies. Domain expertise is embedded in the product — not bolted on.

### vs. ION Group / Aspect
- Position as overkill for SME traders
- Too complex, too expensive for a 4-person brokerage doing 6 shipments/month
- No need to attack directly — size mismatch is obvious to the prospect

### vs. SAP / Generic ERP
From the video script: "We are not trying to be everything to everyone like SAP. We do one thing exceptionally well — commodity trading and risk management."

Reference to "existing customers who switched from SAP" used as social proof anchor in video.

## Dubai AI Seal — Placement Decision

**Decision made (Oct 2025):** Do NOT put seal on TrackonAI or TradingDocs.AI product pages (yet). Use only on trackon.com corporate pages.

**Correct framing:**
- ✅ "Built by a Dubai AI Seal verified enterprise"
- ✅ "Developed by Trackon Software, verified for responsible AI governance"
- ❌ "TrackonAI is Dubai AI Seal certified" — wrong, seal is for the company, not the product

**Placement on trackon.com:** After the "Great reviews" section, before the TrackonAI product section. Trust flow: Reviews → Official verification → Products.

**Use cases where seal adds value:** GCC/Middle East regional landing pages, RFP responses for UAE/GCC region, corporate credibility in enterprise conversations.

## Sales Communication Style

| Channel | Style | Language |
|---------|-------|----------|
| WhatsApp to Arzu / warm contacts | Warm, direct, first-name, informal Turkish | Turkish |
| Formal proposals | Professional, no-fluff English | English |
| Tenant deletion / negative news | Direct and concise — no pleasantries, just facts | English |
| Contract negotiations | Formal, clauses cited, professional | English |

**Demo framing principle:** Never run a 4th exploratory demo. Frame it as a **closing demo** — onboarding walkthrough, not evaluation. Use prospect's own scenario (real shipment, real LC, real sanctions check).
