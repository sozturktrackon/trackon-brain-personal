Three products forming a complete digital ecosystem:

   - Deploy in days, not months

2. **TradingDocs.AI** — AI document compliance
   - Automated LC checking against UCP 600 / ISBP 821
   - Cross-document validation (LC vs. Bill of Lading vs. Certificate of Origin)

   - Sanctions/AML screening
   - Reduces first-presentation rejection from 70% to near-zero

3. **Trackon Legacy** — Enterprise CTRM (proving ground)
   - 20+ years serving large traders (Mana $700M revenue)
