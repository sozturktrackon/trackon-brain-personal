## Relationship Context

Agrisar is Trackon's longest-standing customer, running Trackon Legacy as their primary CTRM platform. The relationship is commercially significant but has become legally and operationally complex due to ongoing disputes around data access, IP rights, and third-party developer involvement.

## Contract Notes

- Currently under MSA + ISV Partner Agreement negotiation
- Legal counsel: ALBM Excello Law (Richard Ding) representing Trackon
- **12-month legacy pricing grace period** post-MSA signature — negotiation concession to secure sign-off
- Historical arrangement was informal / lightweight service agreement — being replaced by formal MSA

## Key History

- Long-standing customer with deep reliance on Trackon Legacy
- Dispute escalated when Agrisar requested Oracle DB schema access for their third-party developer to build custom reports and integrations
- Trackon declined per CSV-only export policy — data is exportable, schema is proprietary IP
- Agrisar pushed back, arguing they own "their system" — Trackon position is they own their data, Trackon owns the application and schema
- MSA rollout specifically designed to codify these boundaries
