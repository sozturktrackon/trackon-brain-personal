# Droplinked (Shopsadiq Ltd.) — MSA Termination

## Summary

Mutual termination of the TradingDocs.AI MSA between Trackon Software FZCO and Shopsadiq Ltd. dba droplinked, effective **30 April 2026**.

- **Original MSA**: 15 June 2025 (TradingDocs.AI platform, ISV-style)
- **Termination Agreement signed**: 30 April 2026 via DocuSign (Envelope ID 46067CD9-0703-80BB-83DD-9A295FA99CF8)
- **Signatories**: Murat S. Ozturk (Trackon CEO) / Ali Sammour (droplinked CEO)
- **Counterparty**: Shopsadiq Ltd. dba droplinked, ADGM entity, Al Khatem Tower Floor 16, Abu Dhabi
- **Outcome**: Clean break. No fees ever owed. Mutual release of past claims.

## Why it ended

Partnership was dormant — no End User subscriptions activated, no fees payable. Already flagged in [[products/tradingdocs/tradingdocs-overview|tradingdocs-overview]] as dormant in the old pitch deck issues list. Mutual termination chosen in lieu of letting the Initial Term lapse.

## §3.5 Non-Compete — Actual Scope (read MSA on 2026-05-08)

**Duration:** 18 months post-termination → **expires 30 October 2027**

### Trackon's restriction (§3.5.2) — NARROW

Trackon shall not partner with, provide services to, white-label solutions for, or otherwise enable:
- Any **blockchain-based, on-chain, or decentralized e-commerce platforms** that compete with droplinked's core business model.

### droplinked's restriction (§3.5.1) — BROAD

Partner shall not develop, market, sell, offer, or implement:
- Any document extraction, document processing, or trade documentation automation services
- Whether developed internally, white-labeled from third parties, or offered through partnerships
- To any customers or prospects

### What this means for Trackon's pipeline

✅ **Fine** (NOT restricted):
- Trade finance / banks / trading houses — Midland Bank, DFA, IMEAA, SGGT, France channel (Youness)
- Logistics / carriers / freight
- CTRM / commodity trading customers
- Direct retail customers via Stripe
- Web3 partners outside e-commerce (e.g., trade-finance tokenization, supply-chain provenance) — but check with Richard Ding first

🚫 **Restricted** until 30 October 2027:
- Partnering with / white-labeling to any blockchain/on-chain/Web3 e-commerce platform
- Helping a "Shopify-on-chain" competitor
- Enabling a decentralized commerce protocol that overlaps droplinked

### Edge cases

- "Core business model" is undefined in the MSA. If droplinked pivots into trade-related Web3 (on-chain LCs, blockchain BoLs), restriction could expand by argument. Track what droplinked publicly claims as core.
- §3.5.3 reinforces §7 confidentiality — don't repurpose anything droplinked shared as Confidential.
- Asymmetry favors Trackon strongly. If droplinked ever asks for a "broad interpretation" or a waiver, push back.
