## 5. Short company write-up

### Version A — ~280 words (recommended)

> **Trackon Software — the Trade OS for global grain trade**
>
> Trackon Software FZCO, founded in Dubai, builds the operating system that grain trading houses actually use. Our platform brings contracts, positions, shipments, document compliance, sanctions screening, vessel intelligence, and country-rules validation into one auditable system — purpose-built for the soft-commodities trade, not adapted from energy or metals.
>
> Three products on one platform:
>
> - **Trackon Legacy** — an enterprise CTRM and trading ERP running daily P&L, risk, and full accounting for established grain and soft-commodity trading houses.
> - **TrackonAI** — our cloud-native, AI-first CTRM. Multi-branch, multi-currency, multi-lingual, ERP-friendly. Native Model Context Protocol (MCP) integration in production since end of March 2026, letting traders query their live trading data in plain English from any AI assistant, with per-user permissions and a full audit trail.
> - **TradingDocs.AI** — our AI document compliance engine that catches discrepancies before cargo is refused at port, validating documents against LC terms (UCP 600 / ISBP 821), country import rules across 193 countries, sanctions and AML lists, and configurable GAFTA / FOSFA rulesets.
>
> The product was a pull from inside the trade, not a pitch-deck idea. A logistics manager at one of our customers had cargo rejected multiple times at Algeria over document inconsistencies. He asked us to build a tool that catches them before the documents leave his desk. Since adoption, his shipments have been clearing without a glitch.
>
> We are a team of trading-systems engineers with over 20 years of grain-trade experience — built by people who have read these documents at 2am with cargo stuck at port. Customers operate across the Black Sea, MENA, South Asia, and beyond.
>
> Dubai-based. DMCC-registered. Self-funded. Building trading systems since 2004.
>
> trackon.ai · trackon.com · trackonsoftware.com
