# SGGT Live Pitch — Presentation Script

**Format:** Read aloud while sharing screen. Demo actions in `[BRACKETS]`. Pause markers as `// PAUSE //`. Target 15 minutes including demos.

**Discipline:** the jury has read our written response. Do NOT re-explain what they already know. Narrate only what they can see now or what's new.

---

## DELIVERY NOTES

- Read at a measured pace, around 140-150 words per minute.
- Look up at the camera at every paragraph break.
- Don't read while doing complex demo clicks. Click, then read the line.
- If a demo fails: "Brief network issue, let me show you the same thing from a different angle." Switch to backup tenant or static screenshot. Never panic.
- End on the ASK. Never trail off.
