## What's NOT in any version (deliberately)

- **"Built by grain traders":** implies conflict of interest, scares data-paranoid SMEs.
- **"First CTRM with MCP":** softened per founder's request, can't verify across global market.
- **"Your cargo won't get refused":** legal liability if anything is missed.
- **"Self-funded, Profitable":** cut as not interesting to grain audience.
- **"Stop emailing spreadsheets":** preachy, treats reader's habits as wrong.
- **Competitor names:** ION, Brady, Aspect, etc. Never named.
- **Em-dashes:** replaced with commas, colons, periods, or rewritten.

## What IS in (deliberately)

- **"From inside the grain trade" / "20+ years inside the soft-commodities trade":** establishes domain depth without conflict-of-interest read.
- **"Priced out of the enterprise CTRM stack":** SME-affordability hook without naming names.
- **"Your data stays in your isolated tenant":** preempts data-trust fear.
- All three products by name and role.
- MCP integration described without "first" claim.
- DMCC and Dubai (regional trust signal for IMEAA and GAFTA audience).
- Concrete language: 193 countries, 6 languages, UCP 600, ISBP 821, GAFTA, FOSFA.
