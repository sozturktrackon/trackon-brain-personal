## What PingPong Is

Cross-border B2B payments fintech (pingpongx.com). Founded 2015. $250B+ processed. 60+ licenses including UAE + Malaysia (recent). Embedded payments for enterprises, FIs, SaaS.

Capabilities:
- Receive in 18+ currencies, pay out in 40+ countries, transfers to 195
- Direct rails: SEPA, UK FPS, SWIFT
- Trade finance via MODIFI partnership (B2B BNPL for cross-border exporters)
- Developer APIs, embedded finance plays

**They are a payment/FX rail, not a trader and not a document/compliance vendor.** Zero direct CTRM or LC-validation buying signal.

## Why They Are Not a Customer

- Not a commodity trading house — Trackon Legacy/TrackonAI solve a problem they don't have
- Not buried in LCs/B/Ls themselves — TradingDocs solves their CUSTOMERS' problem, not theirs

## Why They ARE a Partner Candidate

PingPong's enterprise B2B segment = importer/exporter/trader = our ICP.

### Angle 1 (primary): TradingDocs.AI co-sell / referral
PingPong moves the money; TradingDocs validates the LC/B/L. Same buyer, complementary product. MODIFI partnership shows they already lean into trade-finance adjacency. Vault on-prem appliance is interesting for their bank/FI customers worried about data sovereignty.

### Angle 2 (secondary): TrackonAI payment rail integration
HFD/AFG and future SME traders make cross-border payments. PingPong as an outbound integration in TrackonAI's invoice/payment flow could lower customer FX cost and become a sales feature. Slots into existing [[decisions/2026-integration-engine|Integration Engine]] roadmap.
