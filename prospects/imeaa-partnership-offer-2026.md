# IMEAA Partner Spotlight Program — TrackonAI Offer

## Context

Sudhakar Tomar (IMEAA President) and Trackon Software FZCO (Murat Selim Ozturk, CEO) discussed via WhatsApp on 19 June 2026 a partnership through which IMEAA member companies get discounted access to **TrackonAI**, in exchange for distribution and visibility across the IMEAA ecosystem (30,000+ agri-entities, including ~1,600 named member organizations). Sudhakar requested a "spreadsheet matrix with different levels of subscription bouquet" and named the structure a "**Partners Spotlight Program**."

This document is the proposed structure.

---

## The Offer — IMEAA Spotlight Grain Edition

A pre-configured TrackonAI tenant, purpose-built for the SME grain trader. Out of the box:

- **Pre-loaded grain contract templates** (GAFTA 48, 49, 64, 80 starter set)
- **193 country-rule rulesets** pre-activated (configurable)
- **WhatsApp approval workflows** ready out of the box
- **Sanctions screening on counterparties** at creation
- **Vessel intelligence** integrated in shipment journey
- **EN / FR / TR / ES / PT / DE** UI live; Arabic on roadmap
- **MCP integration**: Claude / ChatGPT query access to live trading data, per-user permissions, full audit trail

Standard TrackonAI requires customer configuration. IMEAA Spotlight Edition is ready to trade on day one.

**Discount headline:** up to **83% off list** at Professional tier for the Founding-50.

**Founding-50 promo:** the first 50 IMEAA companies to enroll lock the Founding price for 24 months. After 50 enrollees, IMEAA Standard pricing applies to new members. Creates urgency, rewards IMEAA for early channel push.

### Why the shipment cap

The included-shipment cap (15 / 50) keeps the bundle profitable on average SME usage. Higher-volume members pay a fair $5 overage per shipment, which covers our external-API cost (~$2.50 per shipment for vessel intelligence + tracking + sanctions) and leaves a workable margin. Members who consistently exceed the cap can upgrade to the next tier or move to Spotlight Enterprise.

## What IMEAA brings (the "Spotlight" side)

The Founding-50 / Spotlight pricing is unlocked by IMEAA's distribution commitments:

1. **Newsletter feature**: TrackonAI introduced to IMEAA's 30,000+ ecosystem in the monthly newsletter (one feature article + recurring listings)
2. **Three co-hosted webinars per year**: TrackonAI + IMEAA, targeted at member SMEs (recorded, distributed)
3. **Co-branded landing page** at imeaa.org/trackonai (or similar) with direct sign-up flow
4. **Featured booth / speaking slot** at IMEAA-DMCC 2027 and any other major IMEAA-organized events
5. **Direct introductions** from Sudhakar / IMEAA leadership to the top 100 grain-trading members
6. **GAFTA, IGTC, IGC channel awareness**: mention in joint communications where appropriate

Without these distribution commitments, the discount tier doesn't unlock; the standard tier applies.

## What Trackon brings

1. **The IMEAA Spotlight Grain Edition tenant** (pre-configured as above)
2. **24-hour go-live** for any IMEAA member (vs. our standard onboarding window)
3. **IMEAA Partner badge** on the platform UI and on member companies' Trackon dashboards
4. **Dedicated success channel** during the Founding-50 wave (WhatsApp + email)
5. **Co-marketing assets**: case studies, social posts, banner ad pack for IMEAA channels
6. **Roadmap voice**: IMEAA gets a quarterly briefing and feature-request channel

## Referral / revenue share (optional, to discuss)

If IMEAA prefers, the discount structure can be paired with a **referral fee** of 15–20% on first-year subscription revenue from each enrolled member, in lieu of (or in addition to) the discount. We are flexible. Recommend discussing in-person whether IMEAA prefers:

- **Discount only** (members pay less, no fee to IMEAA)
- **Referral fee only** (members pay list, IMEAA earns 20% on each)
- **Hybrid** (members pay Spotlight price, IMEAA earns 15% on top)
